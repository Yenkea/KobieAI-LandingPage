Deep dives into how KobieAI transformed businesses across industries.

NordiTech Manufacturing

AI-driven quality control and production optimization

The Challenge

NordiTech, a mid-sized automotive parts manufacturer, was struggling with a 12% defect rate in their production line. Manual visual inspection was slow, inconsistent, and bottlenecked throughput.

Our Solution

We deployed a computer vision system across 6 production lines, powered by custom-trained ML models. The system detects defects in real time, flags anomalies, and integrates with existing ERP for automatic reporting.

76%Fewer Defects
35%Less Downtime
$2.4MAnnual Savings

GreenLeaf Retail

Predictive analytics and demand forecasting

The Challenge

GreenLeaf, a grocery chain with 48 stores across Central Europe, was losing millions to overstocking and waste. Their legacy forecasting system couldn't handle seasonal fluctuations.

Our Solution

We built a custom ML forecasting engine that ingests 3 years of historical sales, weather data, and local event calendars. The system generates daily replenishment recommendations per store.

28%Less Waste
15%Revenue Uplift
$3.1MAnnual Savings

AeroLogis Logistics

Real-time route optimization and fleet management

The Challenge

AeroLogis operates a fleet of 120 delivery vehicles across the Czech Republic and Slovakia. Manual route planning was causing 18% fuel waste and missed delivery windows.

Our Solution

We deployed a real-time route optimization engine that considers traffic, weather, delivery windows, and vehicle capacity. The system dynamically re-routes drivers when conditions change.

22%Fuel Reduction
30%Faster Deliveries
$1.8MAnnual Savings
